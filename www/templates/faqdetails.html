When does the Fantasy Cricket Game start?
The Fantasy Cricket game starts as soon as the deadline for the round ends. The deadline (denoted by the countdown ticker) is usually 1 hour before the start of the first match in the round.
Do you make changes to the pricing of players on Dream 11?
Yes, we review our player pricing from time to time. To be fair to all our users, we won’t make these changes while a match’s contests are open and getting filled.
What if one of my chosen cricketers do not play in the match?
Need to choose your team carefully! The player who does not feature in the starting 11 in the match does not get any points.

How many players are needed to create a team?
You need to select 11 players to create your team with a minimum of 3 bowlers, 3 batsmen, 1 all-rounder and 1 wicket-keeper, while the other 3 players can be from any category.
Can I choose players only from one side?
A maximum of 7 players can be selected from each side.

When do you update the squads for matches on Dream 11?
The squads for matches featured on Dream11 can keep getting updated till the deadline for a match as per the alerts received from our service providers. Keep checking for such updates till the deadline for the respective matches.
How can I see my team’s rank?
You can click on the match that you have created your team for, click on the ‘Joined’ tab just above the contest options and then click on the 'Show' button to see the updated points and ranks for yourself and your opponents in that contest.
How can I change my team name?
Unfortunately, you can’t change your team name once it is registered. Choose your team name wisely, it’s permanent.
How do I create multiple teams for the same match?
Multiple team option is enabled after creating your first team. You can create multiple teams by clicking 'Create Team 2’ above your team on the contests page.
How many teams can I create for a single match?
You can create up to 6 separate teams for a single match.
Can I join the same contest using multiple teams?
You can join only one contest with one team. However, contests which display ‘Multi Entry’ on them allow you to join the same with multiple teams.
